SOFTWARE PIRACY
The illegal copying of a program is known as software piracy . Since copying of program is too easy , it poses a serious
problem for the manufacturers . Software pirates give up the right to recive upgrades and technical support
but they gain access to a program , without paying for it

While you amy know that copying and/or distributing copyrighted software illegslly is considered piracy , you may
not be aware that even possession of iilegal software is piracy .

TYPES OF SOFWARE PIRACY

End - User Piracy
This is when users copy software without appropriate licencing for each copy . This incldes both casual copying
& distributing among individuals , & companies which do not strictly monitor the number of software licenses
that install , and do not aquire enough license to cover their software installation .

Pre - Installed software
This happens when a computer manufacturer takes one copy of the software and illegally installs it on more than one
computer . We should always look for proper license documentation while purchasing a new PC .

Internet Piracy
This is when unauthorise copies are downloaded from the Internet . If downloads are made available on the
Internet , make sure that the publisher has authorised the distribution .
