There are two types of hackers :
  • White - hat hackers :
    •
These hackers enjoy learning and working with computer systems , and consequently gain a deeper understanding of the
subject . Such people normally use their hacking skills in legitimate ways , such as becoming security consultants .
  • Black - hat hackers :
These hackers spend most of their time finding & exploiting system insecurities . They are sometimes called ' Crackers '
because they pose a real threat to the Internet users .

WHAT MOTIVATES A HACKER ?

The factors that motivate someone who is drawn to illegal hacking activities are not always clear . It is well known , for
example , that a few hackers are motivated by financial gain . Most have other motives .

Here are some of the factors that may motivate a person to become a hacker :

  • Curiosity :
    • Many hackers have stated that they were simply ' Curious ' about computer and telephone networks
    how they worked . They wished to explore these networks in order to understand better how they worked .
  • Spying :
    • Hacking of this nature involves monitoring a person's Internet activities or personal data over a
    certain period of time without their knowledge .
  • Money :
    • Most hackers are not motivated by financial gain . Many professional criminal use hacking techniques
    to make money by setting up bogus e-commerce sites to collect credit card details , or engaging in other kind of fraud .
WHO IS THE TARGET FOR A HACKER ?
  • Internet Security Firms :


Internet security firms are the maost likely target for hackers . The very nature of their work makes them natural
enemies of the 'black - hat' community . Their servers and websites contains the best security , making them a
very challenging target for hackers .
  • High - Profile media - friendly targets


These include the large corporations' sites , political party sites , celebrity sites , etc .
  • Anyone with a Website
    •
Anyone who has his own website - be it a e-commerce reasons or otherwise,is more likely to become the victim of hacking
than those who don't . The website is available 24 hours a day , 365 days a week , and can be at hacked anytime .
