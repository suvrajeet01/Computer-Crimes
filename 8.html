Anyone who is connected to the Internet is any way vulnerable to being hacked or falling victim to a virus . For these
reasons and many more , all users should make themselves aware of anti - virus software , firewall and general
security measures in order to ensure that they minimise the likelihood of falling victim to such attacks by making
their personal computers , websites or servers as unattractive to potential hackers as possible . SOME MEASURES TAKEN TO PREVENT HACKING
  • Installing a firewall
  • Heavy penalties on hackers who are caught
  • Using difficult passwords
  • Stricter laws for computer criminals
  • Using the expertise of the convicted hackers to counter the problem
  • New technology like 'Pilot Network Services' that provides supervised Internet access
  • A monitoring device like 'Netanger'
