TYPES OF VIRUSES

Some special categories of viruses include the following:

Boot Sector Virus: It occupies the boot sector of a disk and gets loaded into the memory during the boot-up process . Once
in the memory it attempts to infect the boot sector of any disk that is inserted in the computer system .

Examples :- Quox , Danish , PC Stone & Joshi


Cluster Virus: It makes changes to a disk's file system . If any program is run from the infected disk then , the
virus gets activated as well .

File Infecting Virus: This type of virus infects program files like" .EXE , .DLL " on the disk .

Stealth Virus : This virus attempts t hide its presence . Some even prevent anti - virus softwares from
reading the part of the file or disk , where the virus is located .
Examples :-Frodo & E-Brain


Worms: Worms are types of viruses that can replicate independently among themselves and are often designed
to read the computer user's contact e-mail list from Outlook Express etc , and then e-mail themselves to every
address within that list . As worms are very destructive they copy themselves from computer to computer.
Examples:-Scalper & Swen

Multipartite Virus : This virus infects the boot sector of disks as well as application program .

Trojan Horse: Although technically not a virus , the Trojan Horse poses a great threat to our computer system's
privacy . It gets installed without the user's knowledge i.e , if once installed it begins to log every key pressed on
the keyboard and saves it on a log file . When the user gets connected to the internet the virus e-mails the log file
to the hacker without the user's knowledge . The key log file may contain credut card numbers , personal
information , or network or e-mail passwords . That is why they are called 'Password Sniffers ' .
Examples :-Backdoor , W32.Azak & Nuker

Macro Virus : A macro virus is written in a programming language inside another document such as word processor
document or a spreadsheet document . Microsoft office applications are targeted most often , but any application
that contains a complex macro language can be used to write such a virus .
Examples:- WM Consep. A & Blood Hound
