Counterfeiting
This is when illegal copies of software are made and distributed in counterfiet packages . Counterfeit registration
with unauthorised serial number are often included inthese packages .

Online Auction Piracy
This takes place in several forms , such as resale of software in violation of the original terms of sale NFR(Not For Sale),
or OEM (Oringinal Equipment Manufacturer) software that is never authorised for resale by a third party .
Online distributers offering special deals use these types of phrases to fool consumers into believing that they are
getting genuine product that wouldn't otherwise be discounted .

WHOM DOES IT AFFECT THE MOST

Software Piracy hurts everyone : purchasers , manufacturers , employees , and many others .
  • Global Economy :
Illegal distribution of software affects economy worldwide . In 2001 , according to the Bussiness
Software Alliance , piracy cost the global economy over US$13 Billion . Thousands of jobs in softwares and
related industries were lost .
  • Users Of Pirated Software :
Pirated software may acrry viruses or may not function at all . Unlicenced users do not recieve quality documentation
and are not entitled to recieve technical support or product upgrades , patches , or updates . Pirated softwares
often lead to version control problems that prevent employees from efficiently sharing files while escalating IT
support costs in organisation .

ANTI PIRACY SCHEMES
In the past , software companies manufactured programs with built in copy protection safeguards that prevented
illegal copying but made installation and back-up difficult>

Today , other anti - piracy schemes are more common . One requires a password , serial number or other code to
be enetred when a program is installed . Most developers print the code or serial numbers on the packaging in
which the installation disk is sold . Anyone who does not have the original packaging or who has not recieved the
code from the original owner may not be able to install the software .
