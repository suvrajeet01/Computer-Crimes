PROTECTING YOUR COMPUTER
There are certain ways by which we can protect our computer from being infected by viruses .
We must follow the Dos & Don'ts to protect our computer system from viruses , tha are listed below .
  • We must not open e-mail attachments from unknown senders.
  • We must install a reputable anti-virus software and run it frequently . Keep cheking for the latest
    versions of the program and download its genuine lisenced version accordingly .
  • We should not use illegal (pirated) software as it may contain virus .
  • Always write-protect the system and program disks as they are easy to use & very effective
  • We must always install genuine softwares & install softwares from original distribution disks .
  • Always keep one set of backup copies of all original disks . This helps in recovery process in case of virus infection .
  • We should check virus related sites on the Internet and keep ourselves up - to - date about the developments
    regarding viruses .
  • Always keep a lookout for any unusual behaviour that the computer might show .

Some but not all of then are listed beloe
  • Is the computer system slowing down ?
  • Are files disappearing for unknown reasons ?
  • Does loading of program takes longer than usual ?
  • Do some program files show unusually larger size ?
  • Are there any unusual displays on the computer screen ?
  • Are there recent creation dates on old program files ?
  • While downloading software from a computer network , we must always download it to a disk .
    Subsequently , we must scan the disk for any presece of virus in the software
 ANTI-VIRUS SOFTWARE
Checking for virus requires anti-virus software , which scans disks and programs for known viruses and eradicates
them . After it is installed on your computer and activated , a good anti-virus program checks for infected files
automatically every time you insert a disk or use your modem to retrive a file .
