COMPUTERS IN CRIME

Computers are used for all types of activities , including criminal activity.Ever since selling software online and
computer networking have begun , criminals have found new and differtnet ways to profit and exploit systems.
The tpes of computer-related crimes are numerous and widespread .
But , however it is essential to be aware of at least some of them listed below :
  • Spreading computer viruses.
  • Illegal coying of software(Software piracy).
  • Stealing the hardware or data (Hacking).
Although computer criminals can cause a great deal of harm to personal computer and smaller networks ,
the Internet gives them a much larger access.

COMPUTER VIRUSES

In general , a virus is a parasite program that infects another progeram called the host program.To infect the host
program , the virus makes copies of itself to harm the host program , or prevent it from carrying out certain tasks.
Some viruses are harmless and others,if allowed to run could pose a great threat or damage to the computer system.


HOW DOES A VIRUS SPREAD AND INFECT YOUR COMPUTER ?

The most common mode of virus transfer is through infected flash drives , floppy disks and the internet . Some virus can
infect the system if we boot (start our computer) with an infected disk.
